Service & Pricing

Setting up your project

Web development and design is unique to each client. We have three different packages available depending on your needs. This is why we have you Contact us first to determine the estimated size and time of the project. Right now we are taking as much work as possible without charging an arm and a leg.

What's included?

This all depends on you. If you have a vision of what you want we will be able to judge the amount of time and work it will take. If you don't have specifics (which is fine, not all of us are born creative) We can definitley run some ideas. At the minimum you are getting a fully functional responsive website built from the ground up that works across all devices at a great price. We want to give you great service and delivery.

What about payment?

We work exclusively with Paypal and keep everything in writing via email. Select your package and Contact us directly to recieve an offical paypal invoice. For all packages I charge half upfront and the other half when you recieve the site and are satisfied. Repeat and/or referal customers may be eligible for an additional discount.

Basic Package

The basic package price ranges from $75-$125. This is a quick single page website. Great for a landing page if your business is just getting started. This can always be upgraded later.

Elite Package

The elite package includes three pages of your choice on one site. Typically they are a "landing" page, an "about" page, and a "contact" page. This is great for a business needing to sell their product/service, or even to simply showcase their brand. The price ranges from $150-$299.

Premium Package

The premium package includes large scale websites with login database information. For more information on this package please contact me directly. For all packages we charge half upfront and the other half when you recieve the site and are satisfied.

Maintenance

The Maintenance plan is available for those who's web services have already been worked on but need updated. This can be very easy or very tedious. Contact us for a quote.
